Crispy Hash Browns

Good, old-fashioned, restaurant-style hash browns.

Description

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Onion
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

How to Make Crispy Hash Browns

Here's a very brief overview of what you can expect when you make Hash Browns:
  1. Shred potatoes
  2. Mix potatoes, onion, flour and egg
  3. Heat oil in a skillet
  4. Cook for 5 minutes per side
